2018 Kia Sportage vs. 2018 Nissan Rogue: Interior & Exterior

Most new SUVs are available with helpful features like roof rails and innovative cargo management systems, as well as premium features like leather upholstery. You’ll find all these in both the Sportage and Rogue! However, the Kia Sportage has more standard features, and more innovative features than the Nissan Rogue.

  • All Kia Sportage models are available with fog lights, but not all Nissan Rogue models are.
  • All Sportage models are available with dual-zone climate control, but not all Rogue models are.
  • Both models are available with heated front seats and a heated steering wheel, but only the Sportage is available with cooled front seats and heated rear seats.
  • The Kia Sportage comes standard with a rear center armrest with two cup holders.
  • The rear seats in the Kia Sportage have a 17-step recline feature.

2018 Kia Sportage vs. 2018 Nissan Rogue: Technology

The Kia Sportage and Nissan Rogue are full of interesting entertainment features like Bluetooth® wireless calling and streaming, and Apple CarPlay™/Android Auto™ integration. They also have helpful safety systems like a blind spot monitor with rear cross traffic monitor. However, the Kia Sportage has a more impressive list of technology features overall.

  • The Kia Sportage is available with an 8-in. touchscreen, larger than the one in the Nissan Rogue.
  • The Sportage’s infotainment system includes unique features like emergency calling, a parking meter reminder, and more.
  • Only the Kia Sportage is available with a Harman/Kardon® premium surround sound system with Clari-Fi™ restoration technology.
  • The Kia Sportage is available with dynamic bending headlights with auto-leveling. The Nissan Rogue is not.
  • All Kia Sportage models are available with autonomous emergency braking and lane departure warning, but not all Nissan Rogue models are.
  • The Sportage has not only a rearview camera, but also front and rear parking sensors.
