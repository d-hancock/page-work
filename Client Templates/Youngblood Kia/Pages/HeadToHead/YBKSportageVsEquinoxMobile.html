2018 Kia Sportage vs. 2018 Chevrolet Equinox: Interior & Exterior

Most new SUVs are available with premium features like leather upholstery, panoramic sunroofs and power liftgates. The Kia Sportage and Chevy Equinox have all three! However, there are several other features available in the Kia Sportage that makes it the more attractive choice.

  • All Sportage models are available with roof rails, but not all Equinox models are.
  • The Smart Welcome system on the Kia Sportage automatically unfolds the outside mirrors and illuminates the door handles as you approach holding the key. The Equinox does not have an equivalent feature.
  • All Sportage models are available with dual-zone climate control, but not all Equinox models are.
  • In addition to having more rear cargo space than the Chevy Equinox, the Kia Sportage also has a storage compartment under the rear floor.
  • Climate-controlled front seats and a heated steering wheel are available on the mid-level Sportage trim and standard on the top level trim, but not available on mid-level Equinox trims, and optional-only on the top level trim.

2018 Kia Sportage vs. 2018 Chevrolet Equinox: Technology

Whether you are looking for tech features to keep you entertained on long trips, keep an extra eye on the road for you, or just to make your daily life more convenient, you will be more satisfied by what the Kia Sportage has to offer than the Chevrolet Equinox.

  • All Kia Sportage models are available with blind spot detection, lane change assist and rear cross traffic alert, but not all Chevy Equinox models are.
  • All Kia Sportage models are available with autonomous emergency braking and lane departure warning, but not all Chevy Equinox models are.
  • Kia UVO infotainment includes eServices including emergency calling, parking meter reminder, and more.
  • SiriusXM® is a standard feature on the Sportage, but not on the Equinox.
  • Only the Kia Sportage is available with a Harman/Kardon® premium surround sound system with Clari-Fi™ restoration technology.
