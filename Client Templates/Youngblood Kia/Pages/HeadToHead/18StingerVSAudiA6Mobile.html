2018 Kia Stinger vs. 2018 Audi A6: Interior

 

There are five Kia Stinger trims, and the four Audi A6 trims. For the purposes of this review, we will compare the two most similarly-priced trims: the 2018 Kia Stinger GT2, which starts at $49,200 MSRP, and the 2018 Audi A6 Premium, which starts at $49,700 MSRP.

  • Both the Kia Stinger and Audi A6 come with multi-zone automatic climate control and heated front seats, but the Stinger also has ventilated front seats.
  • The Audi A6 has leather seats, but the Kia Stinger goes a step above with quilted Nappa leather.
  • The Audi A6 comes with 8-way power-adjustable front seats with lumbar adjustment for the driver. The Kia Stinger has a 16-way power-adjustable driver’s seat with lumbar, side bolster, and thigh support and a 12-way power-adjustable front passenger with lumbar support.
  • The Kia Stinger has more front and rear headroom and legroom, and 23.3 cu. ft. of cargo space compared to only 14.1 cu. ft. in the Audi A6.

 

2018 Kia Stinger vs. 2018 Audi A6: Technology

 

In this section, we will continue to compare the technology features found in the 2018 Kia Stinger GT2 and 2018 Audi A6 Premium. Although there are several features that you can find in both models, the Kia Stinger consistently goes a step farther to make your experience better.

  • Both models come with an infotainment system featuring navigation and Apple CarPlay™/Android Auto™, but only the Stinger uses a touchscreen.
  • The Kia Stinger has a 15-speaker sound system while the Audi A6 has a 10-speaker sound system.
  • Both models include a rearview camera and parking sensors, but the Kia Stinger also has a blind spot and rear cross-traffic monitor.
  • The Kia Stinger comes with forward collision warning and prevention, lane departure warning and prevention, and smart cruise control with stop and go.
  • The Kia Stinger comes with a driver attention alert.
  • Both models come with a 7-in. color driver information system, but the Kia Stinger also has sport gauges and a Head-Up Display.
