2018 Kia Sportage vs. 2018 Honda CR-V: Engine Specs

There are two engine options for both the Kia Sportage and Honda CR-V: one standard four-cylinder engine, and a turbocharged four-cylinder. However, only the base model of the Honda CR-V uses the standard engine, while all others use the turbocharged engine. 2018 Kia Sportage vs. 2018 Honda CR-V: Interior & Exterior

Both the Kia Sportage and Honda CR-V come with the type of features you’d expect from a new SUV, like power-adjustable front seats and optional leather upholstery. However, the Kia Sportage is far more comfortable and better equipped than the Honda CR-V.

  • Only the Kia Sportage is available with a panoramic sunroof.
  • Roof rails are available or standard on every trim package of the Kia Sportage, but only on the upper-most trim of the CR-V.
  • The Kia Sportage is available with dual-zone automatic temperature control and rear seat vents in the base trim, while the Honda CR-V is not.
  • The Kia Sportage has additional storage space under the floor of the rear cargo area.
  • Although both models are available with heated front seats, the Sportage is also available with a heated steering wheel, ventilated front seats, and heated rear seats while the CR-V is not.

2018 Kia Sportage vs. 2018 Honda CR-V: Technology

Technology in cars generally falls into categories: entertainment and safety. In either case, the Kia Sportage works harder to give drivers the features they want for a more affordable price.

  • The Kia Sportage is available with an 8-in. touchscreen, larger even than the one in the top tier trim package of the Honda CR-V.
  • Apple CarPlay™ and Android Auto™ are available even in the base trim level of the Kia Sportage but not so for the Honda CR-V.
  • Only the Kia Sportage is available with a Harman/Kardon® premium surround sound system.
  • Even the base model of the Kia Sportage can be equipped with advanced driver assistance technology, including a blind spot monitor, autonomous emergency braking, and lane departure warning. You’ll need a higher trim package to get them in the Honda CR-V.
