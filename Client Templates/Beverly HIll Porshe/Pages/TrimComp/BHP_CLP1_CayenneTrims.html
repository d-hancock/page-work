Starting at $82,900 MSRP

If additional power and performance is a priority, then the Porsche Cayenne S is the perfect choice for you. Key features and specs include:

All Porsche Cayenne trim level features, plus:

  • 9L twin-turbocharged V6 engine producing 434 HP
