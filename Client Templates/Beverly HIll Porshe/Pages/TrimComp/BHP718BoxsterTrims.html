Porsche 718 Boxster

For those who want to experience the essence of the 718 Boxster, the entry-level model provides genuine Porsche motorsports heritage and performance starting at $59,000 MSRP. Key features and specs for the 718 Boxster are as follows:

  • 0L turbocharged Boxer 4-Cylinder engine producing 300 HP
  • 18-in. alloy wheels
  • 0-60 MPG in as quick as 4.5 seconds
  • Top speed of up to 170 MPH
  • Standard 6-speaker audio system, available 10-speaker Bose® premium audio, or available Burrmester® High-End Surround Sound 12-speaker audio
  • Porsche Communication Management infotainment interface
Boxster Details

Porsche 718 Boxster S

Offering additional performance features and a more powerful engine, the Porsche 718 Boxster S provides an even more thrilling driving experience and starts at $71,400 MSRP. Key features and specs for the 718 Boxster S are as follows:

All 718 Boxster features, plus:

  • 5L turbocharged Boxer 4-cylinder producing 350 HP
  • 19-in. alloy wheels
  • 0-60 MPH in as quick as 4.0 seconds
  • Top speed of up to 177 MPH
  • Variable turbine geometry
  • 4-piston brake calipers
Boxster S Details

Porsche 718 Boxster GTS

If you’re looking for track-ready performance, then the Porsche 718 Boxster GTS is the obvious choice for you. Starting at $82,800 MSRP, the 718 Boxster GTS offers incredible features, including:

All 718 Boxster S features, plus:

  • 5L turbocharged Boxer 4-cylinder engine producing 365 HP
  • 20-in alloy wheels
  • 0-60 MPH in as quick as 3.9 seconds
  • Top speed of 180 MPH
  • Porsche Active Suspension Management
  • Sports exhaust system
