Porsche 718 Cayman

The entry-level 718 Cayman offers the essence of mid-engine Porsche performance, bred through generations of engineering and motorsports perfection. The Porsche 718 Cayman starts at $56,900 MSRP and features the following:

  • 0L turbocharged Boxer 4-Cylinder engine producing 300 HP
  • 0-60 MPG in as quick as 4.5 seconds
  • Top speed of up to 170 MPH
  • 18-in. alloy wheels
  • Porsche Communication Management infotainment interface
  • Standard 6-speaker audio system, available 10-speaker Bose® premium audio, or available Burrmester® High-End Surround Sound 12-speaker audio

Porsche 718 Cayman S

For those who seek additional power, performance, and poise, the Porsche 718 Cayman offers a more powerful engine and advanced suspension. Starting at $69,300 MSRP, the Porsche 718 Cayman S includes:

All 718 Cayman features, plus:

  • 5L turbocharged Boxer 4-cylinder producing 350 HP
  • 0-60 MPH in as quick as 4.0 seconds
  • Top speed of up to 177 MPH
  • 19-in. alloy wheels
  • 4-piston brake calipers
  • Variable turbine geometry
718 Cayman Details S

Porsche 718 Cayman GTS

If track-focused features and performance are a must have, then the 718 Cayman GTS is sure to excite. Offering unparalleled balance and cutting-edge engineering features, the new 718 Cayman GTS starts at $80,700 MSRP.

All 718 Cayman S features, plus:

  • 5L turbocharged Boxer 4-cylinder engine producing 365 HP
  • 0-60 MPH in as quick as 3.9 seconds
  • 20-in alloy wheels
  • Top speed of 180 MPH
  • Sports exhaust system
  • Porsche Active Suspension Management
