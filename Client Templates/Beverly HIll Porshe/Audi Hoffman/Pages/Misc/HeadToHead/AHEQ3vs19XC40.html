2018 Audi Q3 vs. 2019 Volvo XC40: Interior Features

Both models come with high-end features like ambient LED interior lighting and leather upholstery as standard, and offer other comfortable upgrades like front seats with thigh extension. However, the Audi Q3 comes with just about every comfort feature you could want.

  • Only the Audi Q3 comes standard with dual-zone climate control
  • Heated front seats are standard in every new Audi Q3, and only available as an add-on in the Volvo XC40
  • Although the Volvo XC40 comes with a power-adjustable driver’s seat, the Audi Q3 comes with 8-way power-adjustable driver and front passenger seats with 4-way lumbar support
  • The Audi Q3 comes standard with a panoramic sunroof, which is only available on the highest trim level of the Volvo XC40

2018 Audi Q3 vs. 2019 Volvo XC40: Safety & Technology

The new Audi Q3 and brand-new Volvo XC40 are packed with features to improve the convenience of your day-to-day, such as light-sensing headlights and rain-sensing windshield wipers. Bluetooth® wireless calling and streaming are also standard features in both models. However, the Audi Q3 is available with more advanced technology than the Volvo XC40.

  • Both models come standard with a rearview camera, but the Q3 also comes with front and rear parking sensors
  • The Volvo model comes standard with an 8-speaker sound system while the Audi comes with a 10-speaker sound system
  • The Audi Q3 is available with the MMI® Navigation plus package, which adds navigation with Audi connect® services such as real-time traffic and weather updates, a parking spot locater, and more
  • Cruise control with coast, resume, and accelerate features is standard on all Q3 models and optional on the Volvo XC40
