2018 Audi Q3 vs. 2019 BMW X3: Interior Features

A luxurious cabin defines a luxury vehicle. Although the BMW is available with many high-end features, you must purchase an upper level trim or optional package to get them, same as you would with a non-luxury model. The Audi Q3 comes standard with many of the luxury features drivers in the Schaumburg, IL area desire.

  • Leather upholstery is standard on the Audi Q3 but not on the BMW X3.
  • Heated front seats are standard on the Audi Q3, but require an upper level trim package on the BMW X3.
  • A panoramic sunroof is standard on the Audi Q3 and an added-cost feature on the BMW X3.

2018 Audi Q3 vs. 2019 BMW X3: Safety & Technology

The new Audi Q3 and BMW X3 provide drivers with a variety of advanced technology, from rain-sensing windshield wipers to parking cameras and sensors. However, our experts believe shoppers will be more impressed by the offerings of the new Audi Q3 than the BMW X3.

  • The Audi Q3’s navigation system includes Google Earth overlays and automatic rerouting based on real-time traffic conditions.
  • The Audi Q3 is available with a BOSE® surround sound system with AudioPilot® noise compensation.
  • Through Audi connect®, Audi Q3 owners enjoy segment-exclusive onboard 4G Wi-Fi® for up to eight devices.
