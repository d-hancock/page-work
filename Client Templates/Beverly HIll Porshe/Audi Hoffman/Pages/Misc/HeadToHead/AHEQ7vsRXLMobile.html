2018 Audi Q7 vs. 2018 Lexus RXL: Comfort

Both models come with standard luxury features such as leather upholstery and three-zone climate control to improve passenger comfort. However, the Audi Q7 is more luxurious and comfortable overall.

  • The third row of the Audi Q7 is significantly roomier than the third row of the Lexus RX 350L. The Q7 has 1.1-in. more rear headroom, 3.7-in. more rear shoulder room, and 5.7-in. more rear legroom!
  • Although heated seats are available on the Lexus RXL, they come standard on the Audi Q7.
  • Both models have 8-way adjustable driver’s seats, but the Audi Q7 adds standard 4-way lumbar adjustment and is also available with 18-way adjustable front seats with a massage function!
  • Only the Audi Q7 offers four-zone climate control.

2018 Audi Q7 vs. 2018 Lexus RXL: Technology

The Audi Q7 and Lexus RXL both offer a wide range of advanced technology, from adaptive cruise control and lane keeping to large infotainment screens with touchpads and voice control. However, there are several features of the Audi Q7 that are far more impressive than those found in the Lexus RXL.

  • The Lexus RXL is available with 9, 12, and 15-speaker sound systems. The Audi Q7 is available with 10, 19, and 23-speaker sound systems.
  • The Audi Q7 is available with a 12.3-in. fully digital instrument panel. The Lexus RXL is not.
  • Only the Audi Q7 is available with a night vision camera with pedestrian and large animal detection.
  • The Audi Q7 is Android Auto™ and Apple CarPlay™ compatible while the Lexus RXL is not.
