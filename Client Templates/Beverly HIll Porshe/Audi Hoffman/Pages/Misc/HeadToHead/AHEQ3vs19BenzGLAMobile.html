Both the Audi Q3 and Mercedes-Benz GLA have enough space for five passengers, but the rear seats of the new Q3 are far more comfortable than the rear seats of the GLA. The Q3 also has more cargo carrying capacity than the GLA.

2018 Audi Q3 vs. 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLA: Interior Features

Both models come with high-end features like dual-zone climate control and power-adjustable front seats with lumbar support to make the cabin more comfortable. However, the Audi Q3 goes a lot farther to ensure the driver and passengers are comfortable straight from the start.

  • Leather upholstery is standard on the Audi Q3 and optional on the Mercedes-Benz GLA
  • Heated front seats are standard on the Audi Q3 and optional on the Mercedes-Benz GLA
  • A panoramic sunroof is standard on the Audi Q3 and optional on the Mercedes-Benz GLA

2018 Audi Q3 vs. 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLA: Safety & Technology

The new Audi Q3 and brand-new Mercedes-Benz GLA have a variety of technology that runs the gamut from light and rain sensing headlights and wipers to premium surround sound systems. But all told, Audi provides more to drivers than the Mercedes-Benz GLA.

  • The Audi Q3 comes with both a backup camera and front/rear parking sensors. The later is an added-cost feature on the Mercedes-Benz GLA.
  • The Audi Q3 is available with the MMI® Navigation plus package, which adds navigation with Audi connect® services such as real-time traffic and weather updates, a parking spot locator, and more
  • The Audi Q3 comes standard with SiriusXM® satellite radio, but this is an optional feature on the Mercedes-Benz GLA.
