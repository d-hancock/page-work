Towing with the 2019 Chevy Silverado 1500 | Hubbard, OH

Do you need a pickup that can tow a large trailer? The new 2019 Chevy Silverado 1500 puts the towing experience at the forefront. Several new features have been introduced with the new model year to make every aspect of towing easier and safer.

Chevrolet erases the guesswork involved in towing. Every new 2019 Chevrolet 1500 pickup comes with an industry-first trailering label to help drivers calculate the weight of truck and trailer combinations. It does this by providing information such as gross vehicle weight rating, gross combined weight, maximum tongue weight, maximum payload and other key weight ratings.

The new Chevrolet Silverado 1500 makes towing high-tech with the addition of new myChevrolet® smartphone app functions as well. These include a pre-departure step-by-step towing checklist, a glossary of towing terms, and a trailer light test for those less familiar with the towing process.

New trailering equipment on the 2019 Chevrolet Silverado 1500 includes the Advanced Trailering System. This system has a Hitch View camera, an industry-first auto parking brake assist, and a trailer tire pressure monitor. Another new industry-first trailering feature available on the new Silverado is a trailer theft alert.
