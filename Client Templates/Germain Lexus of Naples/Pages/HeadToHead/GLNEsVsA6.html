2019 Lexus ES vs. 2018 Audi A6: Interior Comfort

The Lexus ES has more headroom and legroom than the Audi A6 in both rows of seats, as well as more trunk space, making it both roomier and more convenient. Both models are equipped with innovative features like a power-adjustable steering wheel column, multi-zone climate control, ambient lighting, and a rear sunshade. However, the Lexus ES is far more comfortable to sit inside.

  • The A6 comes standard with a sunroof, but the panoramic glass roof available in the Lexus ES lets in more light.
  • The A6 has heated front seats, but the Lexus ES has heated and ventilated front seats and a heated wood-and-leather-wrapped steering wheel.
  • The A6 has a 12-way power-adjustable driver’s seat but the Lexus ES has a 14-way power-adjustable driver’s seat with power thigh support.
  • The semi-aniline perforated leather upholstery in the Lexus ES is superior to the material used in the new Audi A6.

2019 Lexus ES vs. 2018 Audi A6: Technology

The 2019 Lexus ES and Audi A6 are full of futuristic technology such as touchpad infotainment controls that react to smartphone-like gestures such as pinches and swipes, as well as recognize handwritten commands. Both are also available with color Head-Up Displays with speed and navigation information. But by far, the Lexus ES has the superior technology features.

  • The Lexus ES is available with a 12.3-in. navigation display with cloud-based updates, while the Audi A6 has an 8-in. navigation display.
  • Lexus Enform Destination Assist provides 24-hr access to a live response center agent to help you locate addresses and nearby points of interest.
  • Both models are available with Apple CarPlay™ integration, but only the Lexus ES is available with Amazon Alexa integration.
  • The Lexus ES’s 17-speaker, 1,800-watt Mark Levinson® PurePlay sound system is far superior to the Audi A6’s 14-speaker, 630-watt BOSE® sound system.
  • The Lexus ES is available with wireless smartphone charging, but the Audi A6 is not.
  • The Lexus Safety System+ 2.0 includes a pre-collision system with pedestrian detection, all-speed dynamic radar cruise control with lane tracing assist, lane departure alert with steering assist, road sign information in the Head-Up Display, and intelligent high beams
