2019 Lexus ES vs. 2019 Acura TLX: Interior Comfort

The Lexus ES has more headroom than the Acura TLX, the best rear legroom in its class, and more cargo space than the TLX. Feature-wise, both the Lexus ES and Acura TLX keep passengers comfortable, with heated and ventilated front seats, soft leather upholstery, and driver’s seats with extendable thigh support. However, the Lexus ES goes further to ensure passenger comfort.

  • The TLX comes standard with a power moonroof, but the panoramic glass roof available in the Lexus ES lets in more light.
  • The TLX has a 12-way power-adjustable driver’s seat but the Lexus ES has a 14-way power-adjustable driver’s seat with memory.
  • A heated wood-and-leather steering wheel is available in the ES, but not the TLX.
  • Only the Lexus ES is available with a power rear sunshade.

2019 Lexus ES vs. 2019 Acura TLX: Technology

The newest Lexus ES and Acura TLX come with some of the industry’s best safety technology systems, including adaptive cruise control, automatic emergency braking, lane keeping, blind spot monitoring, a panoramic view parking camera, and more. However, the Lexus ES has more impressive technology across all categories.

  • The Lexus ES is available with a 12.3-in. navigation display with cloud-based updates, while the Acura TLX has an 8-in. navigation display.
  • Lexus Enform Destination Assist provides 24-hr access to a live response center agent to help you locate addresses and nearby points of interest.
  • The Lexus ES is available with a full-color 10.2-in. Head-Up Display.
  • Only the Lexus ES is available with wireless smartphone charging.
  • Both models are available with Apple CarPlay™ integration, but only the Lexus ES is available with Amazon Alexa integration.
  • The Lexus ES is available with a 17-speaker, 1,800-watt Mark Levinson® PurePlay sound system while the top-tier TLX package gets a 10-speaker ELS Studio® sound system.
