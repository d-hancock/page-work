2018 Lexus RX vs. 2018 Mercedes-Benz GLC SUV: Interior Features

When you purchase a luxury SUV, you expect premium materials, unique convenience features, and highly advanced technology. Both the Lexus RX and Mercedes-Benz GLC are available with power-adjustable heated and ventilated front seats, power-folding rear seats, and a panoramic sunroof. The Lexus RX is also available with several other desirable features, especially when compared to the GLC.

  • The Lexus RX is available with a racecar-inspired digital instrument cluster
  • The Mercedes-Benz GLC comes with a standard 7-in. color display or an available 8.3-in. color display; the Lexus RX is available with a 12.3-in. high-resolution color display
  • The Mercedes-Benz GLC is available with a 13-speaker, 590-watt Burmester® premium surround sound system; the Lexus RX is available with a 15-speaker, 835-watt Mark Levinson® premium surround sound system
  • The Lexus RX comes standard with self-gripping cup holders
  • The Lexus RX is available with Espresso Walnut wood, Matte Bamboo, or Matte Linear Dark Mocha Wood interior trim
