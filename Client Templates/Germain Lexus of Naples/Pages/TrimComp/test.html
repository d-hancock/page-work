2019 Lexus NX Trim Differences | Bonita Springs, FL

If you are shopping for a new luxury compact crossover, the recent release of the 2019 Lexus NX provides several attractive new options. There are three Lexus NX trim levels to choose from: the NX 300, NX 300h, and NX 300 F Sport. Each of these Lexus NX configurations has a unique performance, so our experts have put together this trim comparison to help shoppers determine which Lexus NX option is the right one for their needs. You can browse all available NX models at our luxury dealership near Bonita Springs, FL by viewing our online inventory, or contact our team for more information.

2019 Lexus NX 300

The standard version of the Lexus NX starts at $36,185 MSRP.

Performance

The NX 300 is powered by a 2.0L turbocharged I4 engine rated at 235 HP and 258 lb.-ft. of torque. It gets 22 MPG city/28 MPG highway, and is available with either FWD or AWD. It also has three drive modes: Normal, Sport, and ECO.

Features

The NX 300 comes standard with a variety of features. The Lexus Enform infotainment system includes Wi-Fi™, an app suite, voice command, and a touchpad controller. Dual-zone climate control, a 10-way power-adjustable driver’s seat, and an 8-way power-adjustable front passenger seat keep passengers comfortable. The Lexus Safety System+ adds advance driver assistance features like adaptive cruise control with lane keeping and automatic braking.

2019 Lexus NX 300h

The hybrid version of the Lexus NX starts at $38,535 MSRP.

Performance

This version of the Lexus NX is powered by a combination of 2.5L I4 engine and electric motor. It gets 194 total system horsepower and achieves 33 MPG city/30 MPG highway, with a class-leading 31 MPG combined. It also adds an EV mode, which allows drivers to operate solely with battery power for short distances and at low speeds.

The NX 300h is available exclusively with electronic all-wheel drive. It also gets an active damping control system, and can tow up to 1,500 lbs.

Features

The NX 300h is equipped with a safety feature that makes a noise to alert pedestrians and bicyclists when the vehicle is operating in (otherwise silent) EV mode. The driver information display also includes an energy flow monitor.

2019 Lexus NX 300 F SPORT

The performance-tuned version of the Lexus NX starts at $38,575 MSRP.

Performance

The NX 300 F SPORT has the same powertrain and engine specs as the NX 300, but improved handling thanks to front and rear performance dampers, a sport-tuned suspension, and an optional adaptive variable suspension. The NX 300 F SPORT also adds paddle shifters and three new drive modes to add more driver enjoyment. Drive modes include: Custom, Sport S, and Sport S+.

Features

The NX 300 F SPORT’s unique styling includes an F SPORT exclusive grille and badges, sport seats with contrast stitching, a black headliner, and unique wheels. It also adds a G-Force display and Turbo Boost display to the instrument cluster.

2019 NX Options and Packages


The new Lexus NX is available with several add-ons, including a power liftgate, power folding and reclining rear seats, and driver assistance features like intuitive parking assist. Other add-on options are bundled into packages. Available packages are listed below.

Navigation Package (all trims)

Navigation system with 10.3-in. display, 10-speaker sound system, and Lexus Enform Destination Assist

Comfort Package (all trims)

Climate controlled front seats, blind spot monitor with rear cross-traffic alert, and auto-dimming mirrors with auto tilt-down when in reverse

Premium Package (all trims)

All Comfort Package features plus power tilt-and-telescopic steering wheel, Lexus Memory System, moonroof, 18-in. wheels, and LED daytime running lights with turn signal indicators

Luxury Package (NX 300 and NX 300h only)

All Premium Package features plus leather trimmed steering wheel, perforated leather seats, Linear Black Shadow wood interior trim, and rain-sensing wipers
