2018 Toyota Camry vs. 2018 Hyundai Sonata: Interior and Technology

Both vehicles are available with a panoramic sunroof, heated leather seats, dual-zone automatic climate control, and other features like an electronic parking brake. However, the Toyota Camry comes with several features drivers will love more.

  • Both models come with a standard 7-in. infotainment touchscreen, but only the Camry comes with a standard app suite including Scout GPS® Link
  • Only the Toyota Camry is available with a Head-Up Display
  • Both models are available with wood or metal decorative accents, but the cabin of the Toyota Camry is also lined with soft-touch material
  • The new Camry has up to three USB ports, more than in the Hyundai Sonata
  • The Toyota Camry has significantly more rear headroom and legroom than the Sonata
  • If you need to install a child car seat, the LATCH connectors in the Toyota Camry are easier to access than those in the Sonata

2018 Toyota Camry vs. 2018 Hyundai Sonata: Safety

The Toyota Camry and Hyundai Sonata have both earned high scores in crash safety tests, so if the worst should occur, you can know you will be protected in either the Camry or Sonata. But the Toyota Camry goes further to help you avoid collisions all together.

The 2018 Toyota Camry comes standard with the Toyota Star Safety System™ and with the Toyota Safety Sense™ P suite of advanced safety systems. It’s also available with Toyota Safety Connect® telematics. The Hyundai Sonata’s suite of safety equipment is neither as robust nor as readily available.
