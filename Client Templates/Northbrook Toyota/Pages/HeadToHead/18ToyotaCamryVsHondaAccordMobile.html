2018 Toyota Camry vs. 2018 Honda Accord: Interior Features

Both vehicles are available with dual-zone climate control, a large central media touchscreen, heated leather front seats, and other premium features that make every trip more comfortable. However, there are several desirable features only available with the Camry.

  • Only the Camry comes standard with an infotainment app suite including GPS navigation
  • The Toyota Camry comes standard with a six-speaker sound system, compared to a four-speaker sound system in the Honda Accord
  • The Toyota Camry is available with sport seats upholstered in SofTex® with fabric inserts
  • The Camry is available with decorative wood or metal interior trim
  • While the Honda Accord offers a sunroof, the Camry offers both a sunroof and a panoramic glass roof
  • The Camry is available with up to three USB ports, one more than the Honda Accord

2018 Toyota Camry vs. 2018 Honda Accord: 5-Year Ownership Costs*

The Toyota Camry and Honda Accord are very similar in price. The Camry starts at $23,645 MSRP while the Honda Accord starts at $23,570 MSRP. That’s only a $75 difference! However, you will end up paying much more over time when you buy a Honda Accord than when you buy a Camry. Not only is the Camry more efficient and more dependable, it also holds its value longer.
