2019 Toyota Avalon XLE

$35,500 starting MSRP

The Toyota Avalom XLE is recognizable by its chrome grille, LED headlights, and 17-in. silver wheels. It has a dual exhaust with chrome tips.

Features include:

  • Dual-zone automatic climate control
  • Entune™ 3.0 Audio Plus with app suite, 9-in. touchscreen, and 8-speaker sound system
  • Backup camera
  • 7-in. digital driver information display
  • Leather-trimmed steering wheel and SoftTex®-trimmed seats
  • 8-way adjustable front seats with heat and driver’s side lumbar adjustment
  • 60/40-split folding rear seats with center armrest
  • Hydrographic interior trim
  • Push button start
  • Safety Connect® and Toyota Safety Sense™ P
  • Blind Spot Monitor with Rear Cross-Traffic Alert

2019 Toyota Avalon XSE

$38,000 starting MSRP

The Toyota Avalon XSE’s unique styling includes a piano black grille with sport mesh insert, smoked chrome headlight bezels, black mirrors, a black rear spoiler, and 19-in. Black wheels. It also has a dual exhaust with quad chrome tips.

Features include XLE features, and/or:

  • Wireless smartphone charging
  • SofTex(R)-trimmed seats with perforation and Ultrasuede
  • Steering-wheel mounted paddle shifters
  • Genuine aluminum interior trim

2019 Toyota Avalon Limited

$41,800 starting MSRP

The Toyota Avalom Limited has a chrome grille, LED headlights with a laser-ablation design and smoked chrome bezels, a moonroof, and 18-in. chrome wheels. It has a dual exhaust with chrome tips.

Features include XSE features, and/or:

  • Leather-trimmed seats
  • Heated and ventilated 8-way adjustable front seats with lumbar support and driver’s side memory
  • Heated rear seats
