2018 Ford Transit

The 2018 Ford Transit cargo van starts at $32,380 MSRP. Continue reading for the key engine specs and features of this capable full-size cargo van.

Engine Specs

The 2018 Ford Transit comes standard with a 3.7L Ti-VCT V6 engine, but has the option to have two other engines installed. Each engine is capable to haul any load to your destination. Listed below are the key engine specs of the available engines for the 2018 Ford Transit.

Exterior

The sleek and clean exterior of the 2018 Ford Transit is what makes this full-size cargo van one of the best on the market. The attention to detail shines through with the black, molded in-color front and rear bumpers and the black with black surround grille. Other key features that increase the style of this cargo van include:

  • Tinted windows with optional fixed rear-door, passenger, and driver’s side glass
  • Power short arm mirrors with optional long arm mirrors, heat, or integrated turn signals
  • 50/50 hinged rear doors with 180-degree opening or 237-degree opening depending on the wheelbase
  • 16-in. steel wheels with black hub caps as well as optional steel wheels with full silver wheel cover, or white painted steel wheels with black lug nut caps
  • Halogen headlamps with black trim or optional halogen headlamps with chrome trim

Interior

The comfortable interior of the 2018 Ford Transit is highly customizable and made to fit what you need. All seating for the Transit is available in Pewter Grey or Charcoal and your choice between cloth and vinyl. Driver and passenger seat adjustability is also customizable with your choice between two-way manual, ten-way power driver and two-way manual passenger, or ten-way power. Other key features included in the interior of the 2018 Ford Transit include:

  • Front air conditioning with optional auxiliary heater and air conditioning
  • Passenger A-Pillar grab handles with optional D-Pillar assist handles
  • Cargo area tie down loops
  • LED cargo area lighting
  • Black, plastic step well pads

Safety and Technology

Providing you with the best safety and technology in a full-size commercial van, the 2018 Ford Transit is one of the most high-tech and safe vehicles on the road. With an SOS Post-Crash Alert System™ and optional Reverse Sensing System, the Transit ensures the safe transportation of you and your cargo. Other key technology and safety features of this cargo van include:

  • Rear view camera
  • Auto-locking door locks with crash unlocking
  • Audio AM/FM stereo with audio input jack as well as optional 4-in. multi-function display, Message Center, SYNC® with USB port, HD and SiriusXM® radio capability, SYNC® 3 with navigation and a 6.5-in. multi-function color display media hub, and Bluetooth®
  • Optional Lane-Keeping Assist with Driver Alert
  • Safety Canopy® side-curtain airbags

2018 Ram ProMaster

The 2018 Ram ProMaster is a full-size cargo van starting at $32,695 MSRP, a slightly higher price than the 2018 Ford Transit with less of the important features than the Transit has to offer. Listed below are the key engine specs and features of the ProMaster.

Engine Specs

Only available with the 3.6L VVT V6, the 2018 Ram ProMaster does not allow for any customization when it comes to the power you want. This engine produces 280 HP and 260 lb.-ft. of torque, while the Ford Transit 3.5L EcoBoost® produces 310 HP and 400 lb.-ft. of torque.

Exterior

The 2018 Ram ProMaster provides 16-in. steel wheels with optional 16-in. aluminum wheels. The 2018 Ford Transit provides multiple options on its 16-in. steel wheels to provide maximum customization for you. While the Ram ProMaster provides a dark grey grille and bumpers, the Transit includes a sleek black grille and bumpers designed with premium style in mind.

Interior

The 2018 Ram ProMaster simply does not compare to the 2018 Transit when it comes to comfort, capability, and seating. The Transit offers multiple colors, materials, and adjustability options, while the ProMaster only offers four-way adjustable driver and passenger seats with optional six-way adjustable driver and passenger seats. While the ProMaster does offer A-Pillar grab handles, it does not offer the option for D-Pillar handles like the Transit does.

Safety and Technology

The 2018 Ram ProMaster offers a 5-in. touchscreen display with integrated Bluetooth® voice command and a charging-only USB port. The only customization available with this model is the addition of Uconnect® 3 NAV with 5-in. display. The 2018 Ford Transit allows for a larger screen and more technology options when it comes to customizing your cargo van. Also, while the ProMaster has a rear view camera, it does not have the SOS Post-Crash Alert System™ or Reverse Sensing System that the 2018 Transit provides.
