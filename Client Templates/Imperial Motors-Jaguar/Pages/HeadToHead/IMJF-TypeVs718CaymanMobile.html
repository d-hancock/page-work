For the purposes of the following comparison, we will be taking a closer look at the 2018 Jaguar F-TYPE Coupe, starting at $60,750 MSRP, and the 2018 Porsche 718 Cayman, starting at $56,900 MSRP.

Interior and Design

Both luxury sports cars are well appointed with premium options, however only one model provides superior features. Both models include sport seats, however only the Jaguar F-TYPE includes standard 12-way power adjustable front seats with memory. The 2018 F-TYPE also offers leather-trimmed seating with suede inserts. Additional interior and design features with the Jaguar F-TYPE include:

  • Premium carpet mats
  • Chrome instrument cluster
  • Power-adjustable steering column with memory
  • Single-zone climate control
  • Power folding exterior mirrors with memory
  • LED taillights

Engine and Performance

If performance is the most important aspect of your new sports car, then our experts agree you’ll prefer the Jaguar F-TYPE. The 2018 F-TYPE features a more impressive 8-speed transmission compared to the less potent 6-speed transmission with the Porsche 718 Cayman. The Jaguar also offers improved fuel efficiency with 30 MPG highway, compared to the Porsche 718 Cayman with only 24 MPG highway.

Safety and technology

Do you expect premium safety and technology features from your luxury sports car? While both the Jaguar F-TYPE and Porsche 718 Cayman offer modern features, the Jaguar F-TYPE includes a significantly longer list of key safety features. Lane keep assist is standard on the F-TYPE, and only available as an upgraded feature with the Porsche 718 Cayman. Traffic sign recognition and an adaptive speed limiter are also only included with the 2018 F-TYPE. Additional safety and technology features you can expect with the Jaguar F-TYPE include:

  • 10-in. touchscreen
  • Meridian™ premium sound system
  • Navigation Pro system
  • Rear parking aid
  • Emergency braking
  • Pro Services
  • InControl™ Apps
