Engine and Performance

When it comes to performance, the 2018 BMW X5 is no match for the supremely powerful Jaguar F-PACE. While both models have the same engine size, the 2018 F-PACE provides more horsepower, torque, and can accelerate from 0-60 MPH a whole second faster than the BMW X5. Additionally, the Jaguar F-PACE also has all-wheel-drive for increased traction and handling, while the BMW X5 only offers rear-wheel-drive.
