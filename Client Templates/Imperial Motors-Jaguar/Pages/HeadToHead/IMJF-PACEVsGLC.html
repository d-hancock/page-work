Interior and Design

Both the 2019 Jaguar F-PACE and Mercedes-Benz GLC offer impressive interiors and seating for five passengers. However, only one model has a longer list of premium features you’re sure to appreciate. Both models include features such as a power tailgate and dual-zone climate control but only the 2019 F-PACE offers a panoramic sunroof. Additional interior features you can expect from the 2019 F-PACE include:

  • Suede-cloth trimmed upholstery
  • 10-way adjustable front seats
  • Black gearshift paddles
  • Luxtec door trim
  • Satin chrome touchscreen surround
  • Premium carpets
  • Bright sport pedals

Engine and Performance

When it comes to performance, both SUVs provide features such as all-wheel-drive and paddle shifters, but only one engine adds more horsepower. The 2019 F-PACE has more horsepower compared to the Mercedes-Benz GLC. The Jaguar F-PACE also includes an 8-speed automatic transmission, and speed proportional steering.
