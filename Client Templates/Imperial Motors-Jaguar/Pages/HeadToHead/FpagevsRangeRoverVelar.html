Interior and Design

Do you expect premium features and design elements from your new luxury SUV? Our experts agree you’ll prefer the 2018 Jaguar F-PACE with leather-trimmed seating, compared to the cloth-trimmed seats in the 2018 Range Rover Velar. The 2018 Jaguar F-PACE also includes 19-in. wheels and driver’s seat memory, two features unavailable with the 2018 Range Rover Velar. Additional interior design features standard with the 2018 F-PACE include:

  • Sliding panoramic sunroof
  • Exterior mirrors with memory
  • Auto-dimming exterior mirrors
  • Heated front seats and steering wheel
  • Xenon headlights with LED daytime running lights
