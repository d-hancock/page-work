Interior and Design

You shouldn’t have to settle when it comes to interior and design features with your new sports car. The Jaguar F-TYPE is the true definition of luxury, with premium interior materials, and an intelligently crafted cabin. Learn about the key interior and design differences between the Jaguar F-TYPE and Chevy Corvette in the list below.

  • The Jaguar F-TYPE includes standard 12-way power adjustable front seats with memory, while the Chevy Corvette only has 8-way adjustable front seats
  • Only the 2018 F-TYPE has sport seats with leather and suede inserts, the Chevy Corvette comes standard with cloth-trimmed seating
  • Both models include heated power sideview mirrors, however the Jaguar F-TYPE adds a standard memory feature

Engine and Performance

Do you have premium performance at the top of your wish list when it comes to choosing a new sports car? It is no secret that both the Jaguar F-TYPE and Chevy Corvette offer impressive features, but our experts agree you’ll prefer the F-TYPE. The Jaguar F-TYPE includes an eight-speed transmission, while the Corvette only includes a standard seven-speed transmission. The Jaguar F-TYPE is also more conducive to Lake Bluff, IL weather conditions with selectable drive modes for Rain, Ice, and Snow. The Chevy Corvette does not offer a standard traction management system. Additional engine specs for the Jaguar F-TYPE include:

Safety and Technology

If you want advanced technology and the latest safety features available, you’ll prefer the Jaguar F-TYPE to the Chevy Corvette. Equipped with a longer list of safety features and superior technology, the Jaguar F-TYPE will accommodate your every need behind the wheel. Key differences between the F-TYPE and Corvette include:

  • The Jaguar F-TYPE comes with a larger 10-in. touchscreen compared to the 8-in. screen with the Chevy Corvette
  • Only the Jaguar F-TYPE has a standard navigation system
  • Traffic sign recognition and an adaptive speed limiter are features only included with the Jaguar F-TYPE
  • Lane keep assist is standard on the F-TYPE, and only available as an upgraded feature with the Chevy Corvette
