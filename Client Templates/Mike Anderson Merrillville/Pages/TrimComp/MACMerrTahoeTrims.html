2018 Chevrolet Tahoe LS

Starting MSRP: $48,795

Even though the Chevy Tahoe LS is the base model, you’ll get a ton of impressive features to enjoy.

  • 3L EcoTec3 V8 engine
  • RWD or 4WD
  • 6-speed automatic transmission
  • Premium Smooth Ride suspension
  • 18-in. aluminum wheels with high-polished finish
  • Manual liftgate
  • 6-speaker sound system
  • Tri-zone automatic climate control
  • Front premium cloth bucket seats
  • Third row manual-folding 60/40 split-bench seats
  • Leather-wrapped steering wheel
  • 4G LTE Wi-Fi® hotspot

2018 Chevrolet Tahoe LT

Starting MSRP: $53,895

The Chevy Tahoe LT upgrades your drive with advanced technology and premium materials inside and out of the vehicle. The Tahoe LT includes many Tahoe LS features, plus:

  • Power liftgate
  • Bose® premium 9-speaker sound system
  • Inside rearview auto-dimming mirror
  • Front leather-appointed bucket seats
  • Manual tilt and telescopic steering column
  • Memory package
  • Power-adjustable pedals
2018 Tahoe LT Details

2018 Chevrolet Tahoe Premier

Starting MSRP: $63,495

With the Chevy Tahoe Premier, expect the very best in automotive technology and advancements, as well as an upgraded in-cabin experience. The Tahoe Premier includes many Tahoe LT features, as well as:

  • Magnetic Ride Control™
  • 20-in. polished aluminum wheels
  • Hands-free gesture liftgate
  • Bose® 10-speaker Centerpoint® surround sound system
  • Second row bucket seats with power release
  • Wireless charging
  • Heated second row seats
  • Third row power-folding 60/40 split-bench seats
  • Heated leather-wrapped steering wheel
  • Keyless open and start
  • Lane Change Alert with Side Blind Zone Alert
