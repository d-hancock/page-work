2018 Chevrolet Suburban LT

Starting MSRP: $56,595

With the Chevy Suburban LT, you’ll receive many of the features you love about the Suburban LS, as well as a handful of upgrades that will make your drive simpler and more convenient.

  • Power liftgate
  • Bose® premium 9-speaker sound system
  • Inside rearview auto-dimming mirror
  • Front leather-appointed bucket seats
  • Memory package
  • Power-adjustable pedals

The Chevy Suburban is fully loaded with all the amenities you could possibly imagine. The Suburban Premier includes many Suburban LT features, plus:

  • Bose® 10-speaker Centerpoint® surround sound system
  • Second row bucket seats with power release
