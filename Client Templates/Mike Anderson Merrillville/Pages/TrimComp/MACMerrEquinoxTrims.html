2018 Chevrolet Equinox LS

Starting MSRP: $26,595

The Chevy Equinox LS adds on a few more features for drivers to enjoy. The Equinox LS comes with many Equinox L features, plus:

  • FWD or AWD

  • Rear carpeted floor mats

2018 Chevrolet Equinox LT

Starting MSRP: $27,795

The Chevy Equinox LT gives you more engine options and all-around features to ensure you have the exact model to meet your needs. The Equinox LT includes some Equinox LS features, plus:

  • 5L turbo, 2.0L turbo, or 1.6L turbo diesel engine
  • FWD or AWD
  • 6-speed or 9-speed automatic transmission
  • Deep-tinted rear glass
  • High-intensity discharge headlamps
  • Roof-mounted luggage carrier provisions
  • SiriusXM® Satellite Radio
  • 8-way power adjustable driver’s seat
2018 Equinox LT Details

2018 Chevrolet Equinox Premier

Starting MSRP: $31,795

If you’re in need of the latest technology and premium features, the Chevy Equinox Premier is the trim level for you. The Equinox Premier includes many Equinox LT features, plus:

  • 5L turbo, 2.0L turbo, or 1.6L turbo diesel engine

  • LED headlamps and taillamps
  • Outside heated side mirrors with turn signal indicators
  • Roof-mounted side rails
  • 18-in. aluminum wheels
  • Chevrolet MyLink® audio system with 8-in. diagonal color touchscreen
  • 3-spoke leather-wrapped steering wheel
  • Universal home remote
