2018 Chevrolet Traverse L

Starting MSRP: $30,925

The Chevy Traverse L has exactly what you need for a successful daily drive. Features on the 2018 Chevy Traverse L include:

  • 6L V6 SIDI VVT engine
  • 9-speed automatic transmission
  • FWD or AWD
  • High-intensity discharge headlamps and LED daytime running lamps
  • 18-in. bright silver painted aluminum wheels
  • Chevrolet MyLink® infotainment system with color touchscreen
  • Tri-zone automatic climate control
  • Front bucket seats
  • Steering wheel controls
  • Teen Driver technology
