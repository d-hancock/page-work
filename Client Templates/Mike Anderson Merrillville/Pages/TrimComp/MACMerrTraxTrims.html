2018 Chevrolet Trax LS

Starting MSRP: $21,995

This base level Chevy Trax is anything but basic. From comfort to style, there’s something for every driver with the new Chevy Trax LS. Features you can expect on the Trax LS include:

  • 4L ECOTEC® turbo MFI engine
  • 6-speed automatic transmission
  • FWD
  • Ride and Handling suspension
  • Halogen reflector headlamps and sculpted tail lamps
  • 16-in. steel wheels
  • 6-speaker sound system
  • 5-in. monochromatic driver information center
  • 4G LTE Wi-Fi® hotspot
  • Front bucket seats with cloth seat trim
  • Rear vision camera
  • 4-way manual-adjustable driver’s seat

2018 Chevrolet Trax LT

Starting MSRP: $23,895

Adding on even more features you’re sure to love, the 2018 Chevrolet Trax LT gives you the experience you expect from a top-of-the-line small SUV. The Trax LT includes many Trax LS features, plus:

  • Deep tinted glass
  • Halogen projector-type headlights and LED tail lamps
  • Outside heated mirrors
  • 16-in. aluminum wheels
  • Roof-mounted side rails
  • SiriusXM® Satellite Radio
  • Front passenger underseat storage
  • Cruise control
  • Remote start
