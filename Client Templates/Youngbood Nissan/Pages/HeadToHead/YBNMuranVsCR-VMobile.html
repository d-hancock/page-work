2018 Nissan Murano vs. 2018 Honda CR-V: Engine Specs

Every new Nissan Murano comes equipped with a tough V6 engine. The 2018 Honda CR-V has two engine options, but only the base model uses the 2.4L I4, and all other trims use a 1.5L Turbo I4. The Nissan is significantly more powerful than the Honda CR-V, and it’s also more satisfying to drive thanks to its strong off-the-line power and smooth ride.

2018 Nissan Murano vs. 2018 Honda CR-V: Interior

Although both the Nissan Murano and Honda CR-V have five seats, passengers will be much more comfortable in the Nissan Murano. It has more headroom than the CR-V, as well as more shoulder and hip room in both the front and rear seats. Furthermore, the Murano has a better selection of features than the CR-V.

  • Only the Nissan Murano comes standard with push button start
  • Only the Nissan Murano is available with a power-tilt-and-telescoping steering column
  • Although both models come standard with rear air vents, the Nissan Murano also comes with standard dual-zone climate control
  • Although both models are available with heated front seats, the Nissan Murano is also available with a heated steering wheel, ventilated front seats, and heated rear outboard seats
  • The Nissan Murano comes standard with multiple USB ports, including one for rear passengers
