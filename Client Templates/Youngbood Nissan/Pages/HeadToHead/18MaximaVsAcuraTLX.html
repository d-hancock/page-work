2018 Nissan Maxima vs. 2018 Acura TLX: Interior Features

The Nissan Maxima and Acura TLX can keep their driver and passengers comfortable in any weather thanks to features like dual-zone automatic climate control and premium leather-wrapped heated and cooled front seats. However, shoppers in the Springfield, MO area will likely prefer the plethora of luxury features available in the Nissan Maxima to those of the Acura TLX.

  • The Nissan Maxima comes with remote start so that owners can turn on the engine and climate control before leaving the house.
  • While the Acura TLX is available with a moonroof, the Nissan Maxima has a dual-pane panoramic moonroof that covers both rows of seating.
  • The Nissan Maxima’s sound system has more standard speakers than the Acura TLX.
  • The Nissan Maxima comes standard with built-in navigation; the Acura TLX does not.
  • Only the Nissan Maxima is available with a power-tilt-and-telescoping steering wheel
