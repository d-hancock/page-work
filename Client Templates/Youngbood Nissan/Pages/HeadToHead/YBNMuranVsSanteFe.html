2018 Nissan Murano vs. 2018 Hyundai Santa Fe: Interior & Exterior

Both the Nissan Murano and Hyundai Santa Fe are available with popular features like climate controlled front seats and a panoramic sunroof, but the Nissan Murano goes much further to satisfy passengers than the Santa Fe.

  • While push button start is available on the Hyundai Santa Fe, it comes standard on the Nissan Murano
  • The Nissan Murano is available with LED headlights; the Hyundai Santa Fe is not
  • The Nissan Murano is available with a unique Midnight Edition appearance package featuring blacked out exterior accents
  • Both models have folding rear seats, but the Nissan Murano’s have a power-return function
  • The Nissan Murano has a memory function for not just the driver’s seat and mirrors but also for the steering wheel position
  • The Nissan Murano has eight cargo area tie-down hooks and rings to make it easier to keep cargo secure
