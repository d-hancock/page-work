2019 Chevrolet Corvette Stingray

Starting MSRP: $55,495

The iconic Corvette Stingray retains the spirit of the original model and is updated to meet the needs of modern enthusiasts. Key features and specifications you can expect within 2019 Chevrolet Corvette Stingray trim levels include:

  • 2L V8 DI engine
  • 7-speed manual transmission with Active Rev Matching or 8-speed paddle shift automatic transmission
  • Performance Exhaust
  • Brembo® brakes
  • Magnetic Selective Ride Control™
  • High-intensity discharge headlamps
  • Wing-style spoiler
  • Bose® premium audio system
  • GT bucket seats
  • 4G LTE Wi-Fi® hotspot
