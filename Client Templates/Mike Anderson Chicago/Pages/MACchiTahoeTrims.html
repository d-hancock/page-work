2018 Chevrolet Tahoe LT

Starting MSRP: $53,895

Expect even more advanced features with the 2018 Chevy Tahoe LT, including an upgraded sound system and a driver-assist system. The Tahoe LT includes many Tahoe LS features, plus:

  • Power liftgate
  • Bose® premium 9-speaker sound system
  • Front leather-appointed bucket seats
  • Inside rearview auto-dimming mirror
  • Power-adjustable pedals
  • Keyless start
  • Forward Collision Alert

Starting MSRP: $63,495

For the ultimate driving experience, select the new Tahoe Premier. Of all the 2018 Chevrolet Tahoe trims, the Premier is loaded with nearly every luxury you can think of. The Tahoe Premier includes many Tahoe LT features, plus:

  • Hands-free gesture liftgate

  • Heated leather-wrapped steering wheel
  • Wireless charging
  • Keyless open
  • Lane Change Alert with Side Blind Zone Alert
