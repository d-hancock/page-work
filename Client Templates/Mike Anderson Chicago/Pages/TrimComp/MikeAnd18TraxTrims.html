Affordable and all-around impressive, the 2018 Chevrolet Trax LS comes with a plethora of features for you to use to your advantage.

  • 4L ECOTEC® turbo MFI engine

  • Ride and handling suspension
  • Halogen reflector headlamps
  • Outside power-adjustable, body-color with black base, manual-folding mirrors
