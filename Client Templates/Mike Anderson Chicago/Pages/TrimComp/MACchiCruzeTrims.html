2018 Chevrolet Cruze L

Starting MSRP: $17,850

Although the 2018 Chevy Cruze L is the base-level trim, it comes with a ton of excellent features you’d expect from a vehicle at a higher price point.

  • Available as a sedan only
  • Available with a manual transmission only
  • 4L ECOTEC® DOHC turbocharged 4-cylinder engine
  • 15-in. steel wheels with bolt-on covers
  • 4-speaker sound system
  • Cloth seat trim
  • 6-way manual-adjustable driver’s seat
  • Rear Vision Camera
2018 Cruze L Details

2018 Chevrolet Cruze LS

Starting MSRP: $19,400

The Chevy Cruze LS adds on a few more features you’re sure to enjoy. The Cruze LS includes many Cruze L features, plus:

  • Available as a sedan or hatchback
  • Available with a manual or automatic transmission
  • Tire inflator kit
  • Front bucket seats
  • Deluxe steering wheel
2018 Cruze LS Details

2018 Chevrolet Cruze LT

Starting MSRP: $22,195

From audio to design, expect premium features on the new Chevrolet Cruze LT. The Cruze LT comes with many Cruze LS features, plus:

  • Available as a sedan or hatchback
  • Available with a manual or automatic transmission
  • Projector headlamps with LED signature
  • LED daytime running lamps
  • 16-in. aluminum wheels
  • 6-speaker sound system
  • SiriusXM® Satellite Radio
  • Steering wheel controls
2018 Cruze LT Details

2018 Chevrolet Cruze Premier

Starting MSRP: $24,395

As the top-of-the-line trim, the 2018 Chevy Cruze Premier is decked out with advanced technology and luxurious amenities throughout. The Cruze Premier includes many Cruze LS features, plus:

  • Available as a sedan or hatchback
  • Available with an automatic transmission only
  • 17-in. aluminum wheels
  • Ambient lighting
  • 8-way power adjustable driver’s seat
  • Heated front seats
  • Leather-wrapped steering wheel
  • Keyless open and start
  • Bose® premium 9-speaker sound system available
2018 Cruze Premier Details

2018 Chevrolet Cruze Diesel

Starting MSRP: $24,695

In the Chevy Cruze Diesel, you’ll benefit from excellent fuel economy, plus a handful of premium features throughout the vehicle. The Cruze Diesel includes many Cruze Premier features, plus:

  • Available as a sedan or hatchback
  • Available with a manual or automatic transmission
  • 6L ECOTEC® DOHC turbo diesel 4-cylinder engine
  • 16-in. aluminum wheels
  • Deluxe steering wheel
