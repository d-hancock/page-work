2018 Chevrolet Sonic LS

Starting MSRP: $16,170

The base level Chevy Sonic LS is available in sedan form only, but can be equipped with a manual or automatic transmission. You’ll have access to only the features you require and a fulfilling daily drive.

  • 8L ECOTEC® 4-cylinder engine
  • Steering wheel controls
  • Ten airbags
  • StabiliTrak® electronic stability control system
  • Front bucket seats with 4-way manual driver’s seat
  • 15-in. steel wheels
  • 4-speaker audio system
2018 Sonic LS Details

2018 Chevrolet Sonic LT

Starting MSRP: $18,595

The mid-level 2018 Chevy Sonic LT enhances your driving experience thanks to a number of impressive interior, performance, and technology features. The Sonic LT is available as a sedan or hatchback, and can be equipped with a manual or automatic transmission.

  • 8L ECOTEC® 4-cylinder engine standard; 1.4L ECOTEC® turbo engine optional
  • SiriusXM® satellite radio
  • Steering wheel controls
  • Ten airbags
  • StabiliTrak® electronic stability control system
  • Remote vehicle starter system
  • Front bucket seats with 4-way manual driver’s seat
  • Outside heated power-adjustable, body-color mirrors
  • 15-in. aluminum wheels
  • 6-speaker audio system
2018 Sonic LT Details

2018 Chevrolet Sonic Premier

Starting MSRP: $20,695

If you want top-of-the-line features, the 2018 Chevy Sonic Premier is perfect for your needs. Like the LT, the Sonic Premier is available as a sedan or hatchback, and with a manual or automatic transmission.

  • 4L ECOTEC® turbo engine
  • SiriusXM® Satellite Radio
  • Leather-wrapped 3-spoke sport steering wheel with contrasting stitching
  • Heated front seats
  • Power sunroof available
  • Outside, heated power-adjustable body-color mirrors
  • 17-in. painted black aluminum wheels
  • RS Package

Sonic Options and Packages

Are you interested in spicing up your Chevy Sonic? A great way to do so is by adding on one of the many 2018 Chevrolet Sonic options and packages. You have the choice of the following Chevy Sonic packages:

  • Front fog lamps
