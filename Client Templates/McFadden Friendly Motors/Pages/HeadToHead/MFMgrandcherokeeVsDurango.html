2018 Jeep Grand Cherokee vs. 2018 Dodge Durango: Safety

You don’t want to leave anything to chance when it comes to the safety of you and your passengers. The 2018 Grand Cherokee has earned a 5-Star Safety rating from the NHTSA, in addition to a wide variety of other awards. Although the 2018 Dodge Durango comes equipped with a variety of safety features, it simply cannot compare to the Grand Cherokee’s 70+ advanced safety and security features. These include:

  • Blind Spot Monitoring System
  • LaneSense® Lane Departure Warning with Lane Keep Assist
  • Forward Collision Warning with Active Breaking
  • Park Assist
  • Back Up Camera and Trailer View
  • Parallel and Perpendicular Park Assist
  • High Intensity Discharge (HID) Headlamps and LED Taillamps
  • World-Class Selec-Terrain® Traction Management System

2018 Jeep Grand Cherokee vs. 2018 Dodge Durango: Technology

The 2018 Jeep Grand Cherokee and 2018 Dodge Durango have many similar features when it comes to technology, but when compared closely to one another, the 2018 Jeep Grand Cherokee comes out on top. Tech features include:

  • Uconnect® Technology
  • Apple CarPlay™

  • 7-in. Driver Information Digital Cluster Display
  • SiriusXM Guardian™
