Trim Overview

The 2018 Jeep Wrangler JL is available in four impressive trim levels. The Jeep Wrangler Sport, Sport S, and Rubicon models have the option of being either a 2 or 4 door vehicle. The Sahara is only available as a 4-door model. All four 2018 Wrangler models have a maximum towing capacity of up to 3,500 lbs., and are equipped with the standard 3.6L V6 engine.
