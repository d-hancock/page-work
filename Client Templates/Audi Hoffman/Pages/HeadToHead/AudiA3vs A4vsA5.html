  • LED headlights
  • Audi side assist with rear cross traffic assist
  • Apple CarPlay™/Android Auto™
  • Bang & Olufsen® sound system
  • MMI® Navigation plus with MMI® touch
  • Audi virtual cockpit

2018 Audi A5

Unlike the Audi A3 or Audi A4, the Audi A5 is a sportback. This five-door design provides 21.8 cu. ft. of rear cargo space, and 35 cu. ft. when the rear seats are folded down. The A5 has only one powertrain configuration with standard all-wheel drive, but the middle and upper trim levels add some exciting features. These include:

  • LED headlights
  • Heated seats
  • Front and rear parking sensors and top view camera
  • Audi side assist and pre sense® rear with rear cross traffic assist and vehicle exit assist
  • Bang & Olufsen® sound system
  • MMI® Navigation plus with MMI® touch
  • Audi virtual cockpit and Head-up display
