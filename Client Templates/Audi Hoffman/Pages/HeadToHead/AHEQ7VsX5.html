2018 Audi Q7 vs. 2018 BMW X5: Interior

Both models come with nicely cushioned seats and your pick of premium features, such as front seat heating and ventilation and rear seat heating. However, the Audi Q7 comes with possibly the nicest cabin in its class.

  • The Audi Q7 comes standard with leather seats. The BMW X5 does not.
  • The third row of the BMW X5 is uncomfortably small and reduces second row legroom as well. Passengers will be happier in the Audi Q7.
  • The Audi Q7’s standard three-zone climate control lets even back seat passengers set their own preferred temperature. The dual-zone climate control that comes standard in the BMW X5 can’t do that.
  • The Audi Q7 is available with massaging front seats. The BMW X5 is not.

2018 Audi Q7 vs. 2018 BMW X5: Technology

As expected, the Audi Q7 and BMW X5 are some of the most technologically advanced SUVs on the market near Schaumburg, IL. Between the two, you’ll enjoy a wide range of safety and entertainment technology. This includes driving aids, navigation, and intuitive infotainment. However, the technology is more impressive in the Audi Q7.

  • While the BMW X5 is compatible with Apple CarPlay™, it is not compatible with Android Auto™. The Audi Q7 is compatible with both.
  • Only the Audi Q7 is available with a fully digital instrument cluster, spanning 12.3-in. and two visual styles.
  • The intelligent touchpad infotainment controller in the Audi Q7 can recognize handwritten phone numbers and addresses.
  • The Audi Q7 offers a 10-speaker Audi sound system, 19-speaker BOSE® sound system, and a 23-speaker Bang & Olufsen® sound system. The BMW X5 offers a 9-speaker BMW sound system, a 16-speaker Harman Kardon® sound system, or a 16-speaker Bang & Olufsen® sound system.
