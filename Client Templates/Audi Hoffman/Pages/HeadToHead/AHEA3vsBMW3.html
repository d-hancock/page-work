Audi A3 vs. BMW 320i: Interior

Even though it’s an entry-level model, the Audi A3 is generously equipped with luxury comfort features. The BMW 320i doesn’t provide nearly as many features to justify the high price tag.

  • The Audi A3 comes with standard dual-zone climate control, while the BMW 320i has single-zone climate control.
  • Only the Audi A3 comes standard with a panoramic sunroof.
  • Even the base model Audi A3 comes with heated front seats, but the BMW 320i doesn’t.
  • Leather upholstery is standard in the Audi A3 but not the BMW 320i.
  • Every new Audi A3 comes with 12-way power adjustable front seats while the new BMW 320i has 6-way adjustable front seats.

Audi A3 vs. BMW 320i: Safety & Technology

Both the Audi A3 and BMW 320i offer several impressive safety and entertainment systems, including dynamic cruise control and premium sound systems, but by far, the more advanced technology can be found in the Audi A3.

  • The Audi A3 is available with a 12.3-in. fully digital instrument panel.
  • Audi connect®, available on the new A3, includes Google Earth™, Google Voice Search™, traffic and parking information, and even a Wi-Fi® hotspot!
  • The Audi A3’s infotainment system is sophisticated enough to recognize handwritten input.
  • While both vehicles come standard with a rearview camera, the A3 also comes standard with front and rear parking sensors.
  • The new A3 comes standard with Audi pre sense® basic and Audi pre sense® front to prevent or mitigate collisions.
