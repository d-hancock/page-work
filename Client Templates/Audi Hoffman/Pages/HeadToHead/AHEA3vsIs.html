Audi A3 vs. Lexus IS: Interior and Exterior

Both the new Audi A3 and Lexus IS come with premium comfort features such as standard dual-zone climate control. However, the Audi A3 is more spacious and practical; it has more rear legroom, and more trunk space. It triumphs over the Lexus IS in several other ways as well.

  • The Audi A3 is available with full LED headlights and LED taillights with dynamic turn signals.
  • Although the Lexus IS has a standard sunroof, the standard panoramic glass roof in the Audi A3 is more enjoyable and stunning.
  • Leather seats are standard in the A3, while the Lexus IS comes with synthetic leather upholstery.
  • Heated front seats come on every new Audi A3, but are only available for an additional cost on the Lexus IS.
  • The Audi A3 comes with 12-way power adjustable front seats including power lumbar adjustment. The front seats in the new Lexus IS aren’t as adjustable.

Audi A3 vs. Lexus IS: Technology

You can depend on any new luxury vehicle to come with impressive technology. However, Audi has always been among the best when it comes to providing ultra advanced features even in entry-level models. See how much more impressive the A3 is than the IS below.

  • The Audi A3 is available with a 12.3-in. fully digital instrument panel unlike anything else offered in the segment.
  • Audi connect®, available on the new A3, includes Google Earth™, Google Voice Search™, traffic and parking information, and even a Wi-Fi® hotspot.
  • The new Audi A3 is available with Apple CarPlay™ and Android Auto™, but the new Lexus IS is not.
  • The Audi A3’s touchpad controller is sophisticated enough to recognize handwritten input.
  • The new A3 comes standard with an SD card slot and 32 GB of storage. The Lexus IS does not.

Audi A3 vs. Lexus IS: Safety

The Audi A3 was named an IIHS 2018 Top Safety Pick when equipped with the LED headlights, and earned a 5-Star Overall Safety Rating from the NHTSA. Lexus does have a reputation for safety, but when comparing these two models, the Audi A3 comes out ahead.

  • Audi’s trademark quattro® all-wheel drive system is one of the best traction systems on the market.
  • Both the Audi A3 and Lexus IS are available with an adaptive cruise control system that can slow down or speed up automatically, but only the Audi can come to a complete stop and then start again.
  • Audi’s traffic monitoring systems are second to none; the new A3 is available with Audi pre sense® front and Audi side assist with rear cross traffic assist.
  • Both models come with a standard rearview camera but the Audi A3 also comes with standard front and rear parking sensors.
  • The new Audi A3 is available with rain-sensing windshield wipers, as well as automatic headlights and high beams.
