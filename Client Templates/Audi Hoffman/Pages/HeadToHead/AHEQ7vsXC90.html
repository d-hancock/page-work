2018 Audi Q7 vs. 2018 Volvo XC90: Interior

Both models offer luxury features such as leather upholstery and wood trim, a panoramic sunroof, and even heated front and rear seats. That said, the cabin of the Audi Q7 Premium Plus is far more comfortable for both the driver and passengers than the Volvo XC90 Inscription.

  • The Audi Q7 is available with ventilated front seats.
  • The Audi Q7 comes with ambient LED interior lighting.
  • The Audi Q7 comes with a 12-way adjustable driver’s seat, including four-way power lumbar adjustment, with position memory.
  • The Audi Q7 comes standard with split tumble-folding second row seats and power-folding third row seats.

2018 Audi Q7 vs. 2018 Volvo XC90: Technology

The Audi Q7 Premium Plus and Volvo XC90 Inscription both offer a wide range of advanced technology, from the rear cross traffic monitor to the optional premium sound systems with 19-speakers. That said, drivers will definitely appreciate the range of advanced technology available in the Audi Q7 more than that of the Volvo XC90.

  • Only the Audi Q7 is available with a night vision camera with pedestrian and large animal detection.
  • The impressive MMI® infotainment system in the Audi Q7 includes an 8.3-in. screen with built-in navigation and a touchpad controller that can recognize fingertip-written commands.
  • Only the Audi Q7 includes six months of free Audi connect® services including weather and traffic updates, a parking space locator, Wi-Fi® hotspot, and so much more.
  • The Audi Q7 includes 10 years of free Safety and Emergency calling.
